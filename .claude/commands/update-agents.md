# Update AGENTS.md Command

Update AGENTS.md and docs/project_log.md with your completed work.

When you complete tasks or reach a checkpoint, document:

- What you accomplished
- Files modified
- Decisions made
- New blockers discovered
- Recommended next steps

This keeps the project's shared context up to date for other agents.

## Usage

Use `/update-agents` at the end of a session to record your work in AGENTS.md and project_log.md.
