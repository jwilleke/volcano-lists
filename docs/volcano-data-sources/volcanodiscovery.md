# VolcanoDiscovery

**Status:** Evaluated — RSS available but licensing restrictive

## Overview

German-based volcano news and tour company that aggregates global volcanic activity from all 9 VAACs and national observatories. Run by Dr. Tom Pfeiffer (volcanologist), IAVCEI member, operating since 2004.

- **Website:** <https://www.volcanodiscovery.com/>
- **About:** <https://www.volcanodiscovery.com/about/volcanodiscovery.html>

## Data Available

- Current eruption tracker (~80+ volcanoes): <https://www.volcanodiscovery.com/erupting_volcanoes.html>
- Aggregated VAAC reports: <https://www.volcanodiscovery.com/news/vaac/latest-reports.html>

## Licensing

> "Most text and images on our websites are owned by us. Re-use is generally not permitted without authorization."

Contact required for licensing. This limits programmatic reuse of their content.

## Sources They Aggregate

- All 9 Volcanic Ash Advisory Centers (VAACs)
- Japan Meteorological Agency (JMA)
- National volcanic observatories worldwide
- Their own field observations

## Integration Notes

- RSS feeds provide global coverage in a single source
- Content licensing is restrictive — would need permission for systematic import
- Better to go to their upstream sources directly (VAACs, USGS, JMA) where possible
- Most useful as a cross-reference or for discovering which volcanoes are currently active
